Me n Bro

A classic sibling photoshoot. This was taken when my brother insisted we had a family photoshoot for whatever reason. This was at a park right next to the Phoenix Zoo. At first we had planned to only take group photos as a family, but then spontaneity kicked in and we decided to have solo shots and group ourselves however we wanted to in that moment. This was one of the many products of that. He decided we would all wear formal clothing for the occasion. I wasn't particularly against it, but I was too lazy to wear dress shoes, so I wore sneakers (which you can unfortunately not see).
